Taekwondo


Taekwondo, Tae Kwon Do or Taekwon-Do is a Korean form of martial arts, characterized by punching and kicking techniques, with emphasis on head-height kicks, jumping spinning kicks, and fast kicking techniques. The literal translation for tae kwon do is "kicking," "punching," and "the art or way of."


There are six stages of Taekwondo,
1)Beginner ➡ White belt, Yellow-Stripe
2)Intermediate ➡ Yellow Belt, Green-Stripe, Green Belt
3)Advanced ➡ Blue-Stripe, Blue Belt, Red-Stripe, Red Belt
4)Junior Black-Belt ➡ 1, 2, 3 degree of Black Belt
5)Master ➡ 4, 5, 6 degree of Black Belt
6)Grand Master 7, 8, 9 degree of Black Belt
